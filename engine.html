The 3-Burst Alphanumeric Ping

Standard APIs fail when they hit an enterprise Catch-All server because the server is programmed to reply "Valid" to every request, even fake ones.

> SENDING: x7q9_fatal_err@domain.com
> SENDING: z9w2_null_sys@domain.com
> SENDING: p4m8_void_req@domain.com

The V3.2 Engine fires a sequence of highly complex, mathematically impossible alphanumeric strings at the server. By analyzing the micro-delays and exact responses to these impossible addresses, our Mailvah Workers decode the firewall's internal ruleset, allowing us to verify the actual target with absolute precision.
