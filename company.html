Our Narrative

Affordable by Design.

Mailvah is not backed by venture capital. We don't employ bloated outbound sales teams to sell you outbound software. We are engineers, building bare-metal infrastructure for other engineers.

The 90/10 Resource Allocation

90%

Bare-Metal R&D

Most SaaS companies spend 70% of their revenue on marketing and sales. We spend 90% of every dollar you pay us directly on proprietary server architecture. By owning our nodes instead of renting white-labeled APIs from AWS, we cut out the middlemen and pass massive compute savings directly down to you. That is why our Apex tier offers 200,000 checks for a fraction of what competitors charge.
