Privacy Policy

1. Data Sovereignty

Mailvah operates strictly as a bare-metal infrastructure provider. Unlike data brokers (Apollo), we do not aggregate, analyze, or resell your uploaded CSV assets. Your leads are processed dynamically and stored in isolated Supabase storage buckets encrypted with 256-bit AES cryptographic keys tied to your User ID.

2. Heuristic Ping Ethics

The V3.2 Engine communicates with recipient mail servers via Port 25 handshakes. We simulate delivery but terminate the connection before a payload is transmitted. No tracking pixels or unsolicited emails are dispatched during the verification process.

3. Log Retention

Server logs (the results of your washes) are retained for 30 days in your Vault before automatic permanent deletion, unless manually purged earlier by the user.
